The first step in mirrorless cameras the EOS R pairs with a new lens mount for a multimedia camera that can take excellent shots and record the best video. This camera includes an OLED viewfinder for clear bright viewing angles with a high resolution. It also includes an LCD touch screen with a swivel design to suit working from high and low angles. The camera is built to with stand tough environments such as mountain terrain with a magenisum build for a tight dust and mositure resistant seal.

The EOS R includes key features such as:

  • Canon RF Lens Mount
  • 30.3 MP Full-Frame CMOS Sensor and DIGIC 8 Image Processor
  • Dual Pixel CMOS AF
  • UHD 4K Video Recording and Canon Log Gamma
  • Dual Pixel RAW
  • RF 24-105mm f/4-7.1 IS STM Lens
